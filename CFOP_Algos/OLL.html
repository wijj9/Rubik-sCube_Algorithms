Step 3. OLL

Orientation of the Last Layer (OLL) solves the top face by applying algorithms. Beginner OLL (2-look OLL) has 2 steps: Orient the edges (3 algorithms) Orient the corners (7 algorithms) Some of these algorithms were already taught in the beginner method. Other algorithms can be memorized by patterns (which is a common technique for memorizing algorithms) shown in the video.
